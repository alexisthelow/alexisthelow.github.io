About Alexis Low


Alexis Low is a transgender woman and an animal liberationist. She lives in Denver, CO with one partner, one human roommate, eight chickens, and two dogs.

The eight chickens with whom she lives were rescued as part of an investigation into a "cage-free" egg farm in Colorado. You can see the investigation at SproutsStarvesHens.com.

She is a graduate of the Landmark Forum and Advanced Course, and is currently in the Self-Expression and Leadership Program. As part of that program, she interviews other people about herself; you can see the results of those interviews here.

Likes: Justice, coffee, chickens, nonviolent communication

Dislikes: Oppression, weak tea, marigolds, violence
